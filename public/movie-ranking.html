My Favourite Movies

My top 5 movies of all time!

A movie that takes you through the toughest times of life. And makes you believe that life can change any moment and can beautiful than you could ever imagine.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

A film that beautifully picturizes love and passion.

Ohm Shanthi Osana

A feel good film that sourrounds the life of a cute young girl which makes you feel love and simplicity of life

Dear Zindagi

A movie with practical insights on mental health and relationships that one can easily connect with. It takes you through the therapy and feel yourself new.

Godavari

A movie that takes you through unexposed love between two random people when they meet by destiny and beautifully visualises the nature and godavari river.
